## 🎨 **DESIGN PHILOSOPHY: Lotus.ai Minimalism**

### **Visual Principles:**
1. **Ultra-light typography** - font-light and font-extralight throughout
2. **Massive whitespace** - let content breathe
3. **Subtle gradients** - teal at 2-5% opacity, not 20%
4. **No visual clutter** - no badges, no icons spam, no feature lists
5. **Elegant animations** - slow, smooth, scroll-based transforms
6. **Monochromatic base** - black, white, grays, with teal accent only
7. **Numbers as design elements** - "01" "02" "03" as visual anchors

### **Lotus.ai-Specific Techniques:**
- Huge headlines (8xl to 10rem) with mixed weights (light + normal)
- Scroll-based opacity/scale transforms
- Minimal borders (border-white/5, border-white/10)
- Ultra-subtle hover states (5-10% opacity changes)
- Single-color gradients for depth, not multi-color
- Negative space as a feature, not a bug

## 🎨 **DESIGN DETAILS**

### **Typography:**
```css
Headings: font-light (300), font-extralight (200)
Body: font-light (300)
Accent text: font-normal (400) on gradient
Size range: xl (20px) to 10rem (160px)
```

### **Colors:**
```css
Background: #000000 (pure black)
Text primary: #FFFFFF (white)
Text secondary: #9CA3AF (gray-400)
Text tertiary: #4B5563 (gray-600)
Accent: #00E5CC (medical teal)
Borders: rgba(255,255,255,0.05) to 0.1
Gradients: #00E5CC at 2-5% opacity
```

### **Spacing:**
```css
Section padding: py-40 (160px vertical)
Content max-width: max-w-5xl to max-w-7xl
Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
Element spacing: space-y-8 to space-y-16
```

### **Animations:**
```css
Duration: 0.8s to 1.2s (slower = more elegant)
Easing: [0.19, 1, 0.22, 1] (custom bezier)
Delays: Staggered 0.1s to 0.2s increments
Hover: scale-1.02 (subtle, not 1.05)
```
