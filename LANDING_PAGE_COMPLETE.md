**Q1: How does BioXverse access my medical records?**

A: Through FHIR R4 APIs — the same secure standard mandated by CMS. You authorize access, we execute on your behalf. You can revoke at any time.

---

**Q2: Is my data secure?**

A: HIPAA-compliant, AES-256 encrypted, hosted on AWS GovCloud. We never log PHI in audit trails. Your data is never sold or used for advertising. Ever.

---

**Q3: What if my provider doesn't have a portal?**

A: We handle non-API providers through Direct Protocol and manual confirmation workflows. Most tasks can still be completed — it just takes slightly longer.

---

**Q4: How much does it cost?**

A: Free tier (3 tasks/month), $15/month Basic, $25/month Premium, $35/month Family Plan. Early access users get Premium free for 6 months.

## 🔑 KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES

### Brand Voice Rules
1. **Sharp & Punchy:** Short sentences. Direct language. No fluff.
2. **Problem-First:** Lead with pain points before solutions
3. **Proof Over Promise:** Real scenarios, real metrics, real people
4. **Invisible Concierge:** Positioning as effortless, behind-the-scenes automation
5. **Medical Authority:** Trust signals (HIPAA, SOC 2, clinical integrations)

### Copywriting Formula
- **Headlines:** Big, bold claims with gradient accent on key phrase
- **Subheadlines:** Explain the "how" with concrete details
- **Examples:** Always provide real-world scenarios in quotation marks
- **CTAs:** Action-oriented, benefit-focused ("Join the Waitlist" not "Sign Up")
